The Best Beer Cheese Recipe Ever

SERVINGS | Serves 8-10
CALORIES | 150 per serving
PREP TIME | 15 minutes
COOK TIME | 10 minutes
TOTAL TIME | 25 minutes

Equipment
- Measuring spoons
- Measuring cups
- Medium saucepan
- Whisk
- Cheese grater
- Blender or food processor

Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3/4 cup beer (preferably a lager or ale for flavor)
- 1 teaspoon mustard powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional for a smoky flavor)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, grated
- 1 cup Gruy√®re cheese, grated
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Instructions
1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour to create a roux, cooking for 1-2 minutes until golden.
2. Gradually whisk in milk and beer until the mixture is smooth and starts to thicken.
3. Add mustard powder, garlic powder, smoked paprika (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well.
4. Reduce heat to low and gradually add the grated cheeses, stirring until completely melted and smooth.
5. Stir in Worcestershire sauce.
6. For a smoother consistency, transfer the mixture to a blender or food processor, and blend until smooth.
7. Serve warm with pretzels, crusty bread, or vegetables for dipping.

Enjoy your delicious beer cheese, perfect for gatherings or a cozy night in!
